About Us

ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Train to ramen station will be a business in a food industry which will offer a quality yet affordable street ramen. The business will be placed beside/near different train stations around Metro Manila such as LRT, MRT and PNR stations. The first branch will be placed near LRT 1 Tayuman station.

  • The business aims to be recognized for its affordable price among different ramen restaurant in Metro Manila. Using the pricing strategy, this business will aim to achieve a name in the market featuring its quality products, safe environment and price that people can afford.
  • Train to ramen station will be run by four people in a partnership ana aims to target ages 18 to 40 and above mainly students, young adults and professional that are willing to explore Japanese foods.

The main goal of this business is to promote the Japanese culture through its delicious dishes serve in an affordable price. The business will be a place for foodies around the Metro giving them the opportunity to experience the taste of Japan.
